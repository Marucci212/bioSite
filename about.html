About Madison

Meet Madison

Madison was born and raised in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and she completed all twelve years of elementary and secondary schooling in a Catholic school. She went on to study at Duquesne University, earning a bachelor's degree in business finance and a master's degree in business administration, all before the age of 24. She is now a financial analyst for UPMC, one of the region's premier healthcare providers.Her family, especially her mother, greatly influenced Madison as she grew up. She was a dominant figure in both Madison's personal and professional life. Her grandmother also played an essential role in the influence. Her grandmother taught her how to paint ceramics, and she became a creative enthusiast for life. Madison loves attending spin class, painting ceramics, and creating flower bouquets. On weekends or days off, she enjoys spending time with her family and friends, exercising, or reading fantasy novels, and her favorite is the ACOTAR series.Despite her success, Madison struggled after college, not knowing what she wanted to do. Her perseverance, however, paid off, and she is now content with her role at UPMC. Madison has some grand plans for the future. She desires to travel more and experience other cultures. Career-wise, she wishes to lead and manage a team in her field.Ten years from now, she sees herself married with children, thriving in an upper-management position, and investing in the people who have supported her.Madison's tale of perseverance, passion, and determination is very inspiring for other people. Her journey serves as a reminder that it's okay not to have everything figured out immediately as long as you keep moving forward.
